# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream
AutoStream is a SaaS platform that provides automated video editing tools for content creators.
It uses AI to help YouTubers, Instagram creators, and other video producers edit faster and smarter.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- Price: $29/month
- Videos: 10 videos/month
- Resolution: Up to 720p
- AI Captions: Not included
- Support: Standard support (business hours)

### Pro Plan
- Price: $79/month
- Videos: Unlimited
- Resolution: Up to 4K
- AI Captions: Included
- Support: 24/7 priority support

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
- Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase
- No refunds after 7 days from the date of subscription

### Support Policy
- 24/7 customer support is available exclusively on the Pro plan
- Basic plan users receive standard business-hours support

## Features Overview

### AI-Powered Editing
- Automatic cut detection
- Background noise removal
- Color correction suggestions

### Export & Sharing
- Direct upload to YouTube, Instagram, TikTok
- Custom thumbnails
- Subtitle/caption generation (Pro only)

## FAQs

**Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro?**
Yes, you can upgrade anytime. The price difference is prorated for the current billing cycle.

**Is there a free trial?**
AutoStream offers a 3-day free trial on the Pro plan. No credit card required.

**What platforms are supported?**
AutoStream works on web browsers. Desktop apps for Mac and Windows are available for Pro users.

**Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Access continues until the end of the billing period.
